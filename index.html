What is HRS?

HRS is a remodeling and renovation company that specializes in bathroom and kitchen renovations. From kitchen islands, cabinets, and counters to sinks, drawers, and mirrors, we can install them all. Do you have a dilapidated kitchen area or a bathroom that needs a serious makeover? Worry not! You can contact one of our design specialists and consult them for any modeling advice. Or if you're not looking to redo a whole area, you can visit our physical locations to purchase whatever pieces you deem necessary.

What services do we provide?

We provide services related to renovations, restorations, and consultations. Whether you are looking to rework a whole kitchen/ bathroom area, fix up an old furnishing piece, or simply looking for advice for modeling we will be willing to assist you. Below are some examples of our remodeling jobs.
